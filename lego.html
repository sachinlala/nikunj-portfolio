Lego Building

Combining creativity, patience, and problem-solving through construction

One of my biggest interests apart from sports and gaming is building with Lego. I enjoy it as it combines creativity, patience, and problem-solving. I have built several detailed sets, including the F1 Mercedes car, the McLaren car, and even a McLaren duster car. Each project feels like a journey, starting with hundreds of pieces and slowly turning them into something real and impressive. Building with Lego teaches me focus and perseverance, since every step matters to complete the final model. What I love the most is the sense of achievement when I see the finished car in front of me, knowing I created it piece by piece. For me, Lego is not just about making models—it's about using imagination, building skills, and enjoying the process of creation.
